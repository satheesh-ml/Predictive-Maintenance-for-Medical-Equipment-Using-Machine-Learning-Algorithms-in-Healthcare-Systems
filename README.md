# Predictive-Maintenance-for-Medical-Equipment-Using-Machine-Learning-Algorithms-in-Healthcare-Systems

•	Development of a machine learning–based predictive maintenance model for medical equipment failure detection.
•	Analysis of operational parameters such as temperature, speed, torque, and usage time for identifying failure patterns.
•	Reduction of unexpected equipment breakdowns and maintenance costs in healthcare systems.
•	Improvement of reliability and operational efficiency of critical medical devices.
•	Enhancement of patient safety through continuous availability of healthcare equipment.
